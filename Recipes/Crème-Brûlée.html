Crème-Brûlée

Description

Crème brûlée is a classic French dessert that is perfect for a special occasion. This recipe is easy to make and tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  2. In a saucepan, heat cream and vanilla extract over low heat until hot but not boiling.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together egg yolks and white sugar until light and frothy.
  4. Slowly pour hot cream into egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly.
  5. Strain mixture through a fine sieve into a pouring jug.
  6. Divide mixture among four ramekins and place in a baking dish.
  7. Pour enough hot water into the baking dish to come halfway up the sides of the ramekins.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until set but still slightly wobbly in the center, about 40 minutes.
  9. Remove ramekins from water bath and let cool to room temperature.
  10. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 2 hours.
  11. Sprinkle brown sugar over the tops of the chilled custards.
  12. Use a kitchen torch to caramelize the sugar until golden and crispy.
  13. Let sit for a few minutes to allow the sugar to harden, then serve.
