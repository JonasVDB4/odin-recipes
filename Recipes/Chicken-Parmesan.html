Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian dish that is perfect for a weeknight meal. This recipe is easy to make and tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken breasts between two sheets of wax paper and pound to 1/4-inch thickness.
  3. In a shallow dish, combine flour, salt, and pepper.
  4. In another shallow dish, combine Parmesan cheese and breadcrumbs.
  5. Coat chicken breasts in flour mixture, then dip in egg, and coat in breadcrumb mixture.
  6. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  7. Add chicken breasts and cook until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  8. Transfer chicken breasts to a baking dish and top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
  10. Remove from oven and sprinkle with chopped basil before serving.
